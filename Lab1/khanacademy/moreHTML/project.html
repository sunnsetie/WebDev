1. Potato with meat

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: with ketchup or any other sauce
Ingredients Quantity
Potato 1.5 kg
Meat 500 g
Onion 1 piece
Salt -

Step 1: Cut the meat into cubes.
Step 2: Sliced meat fried in a pan.
Step 3: Fry the meat on all sides for about 5 minutes over high heat until golden brown. Turn the meat only after it is fried, it is often not necessary to turn over.
Step 4: While the meat is frying, cut one onion into small cubes.
Step 5: Sliced meat fried in a pan.
Step 6: Then fill it all with water and simmer over low heat for about an hour.
Step 7: Next, we cut the potatoes and put them in a pan.
Step 8: We spread the meat with onions to the potatoes and again pour water to it.
Step 9 Wait 15 minutes and then serve on the table to enjoy the taste of food!

2. Fettuccine "Alfredo"

  • Time: 15 minutes
  • Serves: with cheese
Ingredients Quantity
Pasta 250 g
Cream 200 ml
Egg yolk 1 piece
Parmesan 80 g

Step 1: Grate cheese on a fine grater.
Step 2: Cook pasta.
Step 3: Pour the cream into the yolk.
Step 4: Mix them with a whisk.
Step 5: Heat oil in a frying pan and add this mixture, stirring constantly.
Step 6: Add pasta and cheese to this mixture.
Step 7: Add salt and pepper to taste.
Step 8: Serve and eat.

3. Fortune cookies

  • Time: 1 hour
  • Serves: with tea (optional)
Ingredients Quantity
Butter 60 g
Egg whites 3 pieces
Powdered sugar 125 g
Flour 65 g

Step 1: Chop the butter and put it in a heated pan, stirring.
Step 2: Beat the egg whites and mix quickly.
Step 3: Whisking continuously, add the powdered sugar to the mixture.
Step 4: Again, add melted butter, flour to this mixture and mix. Now wait 15 minutes.
Step 5: Preheat the oven to 180Â°C and prepare a baking sheet.
Step 6: Spread the dough with a tablespoon at a distance from each other and send to the oven.
Step 7: Remove the cookies from the baking sheet and quickly roll them up, placing a fortune note in each dough.
Step 8: Eat and enjoy!
